Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Video to MP3 Converter Pro ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This App is designed to function strictly as a local utility tool on your device.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The App operates entirely offline and processes your files locally on your device. We do not have access to your videos, audio files, or any other personal information stored on your device.

2. Permissions

Minimizing permissions is our priority. The App requests the following permissions solely to perform its core functionality:

  • Storage Access (Read/Write): Required to select video files from your device for conversion and to save the converted MP3 files to your storage. On newer Android versions, this may include permissions for "Music and Audio" or "Photos and Videos".
  • Notifications (Optional): Used solely to inform you when a long-running conversion process is complete.

3. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs that collect user data. All processing logic (using libraries such as FFmpeg) is contained within the App and executes locally on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, our App is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

5. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Since all file conversion occurs locally on your device, your files never leave your possession. We do not upload your content to any servers.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
